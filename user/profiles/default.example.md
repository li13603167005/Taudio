# Listening Preference Example

- Preferred languages: mostly English, with occasional Chinese songs when explicitly requested.
- Preferred artists: The Weeknd, Post Malone, HONNE, Frank Ocean, Imagine Dragons.
- Frequent styles: melodic pop, alternative R&B, indie pop, future bass, city-night electronic.
- Avoid: repeating the same artist too often, low-quality karaoke/instrumental covers, overly generic recommendations.
- Morning: clean, bright, lightly energetic pop or electronic songs.
- Focus: steady rhythm, fewer interruptions, not too lyrically distracting.
- Evening: warmer, more intimate, reflective songs.
- Special notes: prefer concise DJ intros with a meaningful reason for the recommendation.
